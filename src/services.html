Services


The Security Team provides services in the areas of Capabilities Management, Cyber Risk, Fusion Center, and Security Architecture. Our services are always evolving and you will find a brief page providing an overview of each service and its associated activities below.

Services

Capabilities Management

Organizational and business practices that increase security capabilities across teams and deparments at WMF

  • Cyber Risk

    Protecting the assets of the Wikimedia movement by measuring, tracking, and mitigating risk across the organization

  • Fusion Center

    Reviewing software, drafting policies and playbooks, responding to incidents in real-time, and providing security education

  • Security Architecture

    Building the infrastructure, tooling, and standards to keep WMF systems and data safe, secure, and private
